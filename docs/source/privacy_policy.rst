***************
Privacy Policy
***************

Overview
========

This Privacy Policy describes how beeSuite collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our HR software suite.

Information We Collect
=======================

We may collect the following types of information:

- Personal information you provide (name, email, employee details)
- Usage data (login times, features accessed)
- Location data through our beewhere service (with your consent)

How We Use Your Information
===========================

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Process employee lifecycle management tasks
- Improve our software and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure
===========================

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

- With your consent
- To comply with legal requirements
- With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security
=============

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights
===========

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing

Cookies and Tracking
====================

We may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

Changes to This Policy
======================

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes.

Contact Us
==========

For privacy-related questions, please contact admin@beesuite.app.
